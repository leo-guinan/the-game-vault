FAQ

Parents/guardians: you’re the customer. Questions are welcome.

What ages is this for?

Set your intended range here (example: ages 10–14). We confirm during the parent verification call.

What happens on the Zoom call?

It’s a short (≈10 min) parent/guardian call to verify age, review rules, and do a friendly intro to the host. Discord access is sent after the call.

Do parents need Discord?

No. Parents don’t need to join Discord—just an email address to receive updates and contact an admin if needed.

How is the server moderated?

Invite-only access, clear rules, and active moderation. Members who break rules are removed, especially around bullying, harassment, or sharing personal info.

Is this a one-time payment?

Yes — $20 one time. No monthly subscription.
